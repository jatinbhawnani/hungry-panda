About Hungary Panda

Founded in 1995 by Hungarian immigrants, Hungary Panda has been serving authentic Hungarian cuisine for over 25 years. Our founder, István Kovács, brought his family recipes from Budapest and created a restaurant that feels like home.

We pride ourselves on using traditional cooking methods and the finest ingredients to create dishes that stay true to their Hungarian roots while appealing to modern palates. Our chefs have trained in Hungary and bring decades of experience to every dish.

At Hungary Panda, we believe in the Hungarian philosophy of "jó étvágyat" (good appetite) - that every meal should be a celebration of flavors, shared with good company.
